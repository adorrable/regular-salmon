Our Story

Adornment by Ade is born from a reverence for Yoruba craftsmanship and the luminous spirit of 18k gold. Each piece is handmade by Nigerian artisans, drawing on centuries-old techniques, sacred motifs, and the warmth of African sun-lit gold. Our process honors sustainability and community, weaving narrative into every curve and pattern.

Key elements of our craft include:

  • Solid 18k gold with high-polish finishes and dimensional reliefs
  • Traditional Yoruba motifs interpreted for modern luxury wear
  • Small-batch production supporting local Nigerian artisans
